# 2026-05-10 - CAISI widens pre-deployment AI evaluations

The U.S. AI safety evaluation ecosystem expanded its collaboration with major frontier labs.

## What happened

- CAISI and NIST broadened pre-deployment evaluation partnerships.
- The work involved leading labs including Google DeepMind, Microsoft, and xAI.
- Evaluation before release continued to become a concrete governance mechanism.

## Why it matters

AI governance is becoming more operational. Instead of remaining a policy slogan, pre-release testing is turning into a shared process that can shape deployment timing, risk assessment, and public trust.
