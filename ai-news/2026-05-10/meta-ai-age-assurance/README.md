# 2026-05-10 - Meta frames AI age assurance as safety infrastructure

Meta positioned AI-powered age assurance as part of youth protection and platform governance.

## What happened

- AI systems were used to support age estimation and teen account protections.
- Product policy, identity signals, and safety enforcement became more tightly connected.
- The work showed how platform safety increasingly depends on automated classification.

## Why it matters

Age assurance is becoming a core trust layer for consumer platforms. The challenge is to improve safety while managing privacy, accuracy, appeals, and the risk of overreliance on automated decisions.
