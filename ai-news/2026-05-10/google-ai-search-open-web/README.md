# 2026-05-10 - Google AI Search reconnects answers to the open web

Google's AI Search updates emphasized links, previews, subscriptions, forums, and social context around generated answers.

## What happened

- AI Mode and AI Overviews continued to evolve as search interfaces.
- Generated summaries were paired with more visible routes back to external sources.
- Open web signals became part of the product story around AI answers.

## Why it matters

Search is no longer only a list of links, but it also cannot become a sealed answer box. The next design problem is balancing synthesized answers with attribution, discovery, creator traffic, and user trust.
