# 2026-05-10 - Enterprise AI advantage shifts to depth of use

Signals from Microsoft and OpenAI pointed toward a new enterprise AI benchmark: not whether teams use AI, but how deeply it changes work.

## What happened

- Work trend research highlighted differences between shallow tool adoption and redesigned workflows.
- Agentic coding and productivity tools made AI more embedded in day-to-day operations.
- B2B AI value increasingly appeared in process redesign, not isolated prompt use.

## Why it matters

The competitive advantage is moving from access to execution. Organizations that build repeatable AI workflows, train teams, and measure operational outcomes are likely to compound faster than those treating AI as a generic assistant.
