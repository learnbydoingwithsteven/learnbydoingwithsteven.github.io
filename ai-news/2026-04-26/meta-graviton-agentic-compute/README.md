# 2026-04-26 - Meta and AWS Graviton broaden agentic compute

Meta's use of AWS Graviton highlighted a more diversified compute stack for AI workloads.

## What happened

- Tens of millions of Graviton cores became part of Meta's infrastructure story.
- Agentic AI workloads pushed companies to optimize across different processor types.
- Compute strategy expanded beyond a single accelerator category.

## Why it matters

AI infrastructure is becoming heterogeneous. Efficient agentic systems will likely combine GPUs, custom accelerators, CPUs, networking, and scheduling strategies instead of relying on one compute pattern.
