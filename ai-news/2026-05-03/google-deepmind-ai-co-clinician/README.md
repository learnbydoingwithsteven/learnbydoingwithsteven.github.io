# 2026-05-03 - Google DeepMind explores the AI co-clinician

Google DeepMind's healthcare work emphasized AI as a supervised clinical collaborator.

## What happened

- The co-clinician pattern framed AI as support for clinical teams.
- Nursing, care coordination, and decision support workflows became key application areas.
- Physician oversight remained central to the deployment model.

## Why it matters

Healthcare AI adoption depends on trust, accountability, and workflow fit. The co-clinician framing is important because it avoids the simplistic idea of replacing doctors and focuses on safer human-AI collaboration.
