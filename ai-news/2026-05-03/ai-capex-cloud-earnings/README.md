# 2026-05-03 - AI capex becomes the cloud earnings story

Earnings from major technology companies made AI infrastructure spending a central market signal.

## What happened

- Microsoft, Alphabet, Amazon, and Meta all highlighted AI-related infrastructure investment.
- Cloud growth, data center buildout, and model demand were increasingly discussed together.
- Capital expenditure became a visible proxy for AI platform ambition.

## Why it matters

The AI race is no longer only about model quality. It is also about power, chips, data centers, cloud margins, and the ability to absorb huge upfront investment before demand fully matures.
