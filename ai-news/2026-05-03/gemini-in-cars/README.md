# 2026-05-03 - Gemini enters cars with Google built-in

Gemini moved further into vehicles through Google built-in experiences.

## What happened

- In-car AI assistants moved toward natural dialogue.
- Use cases included routing, EV support, manuals, and contextual driving assistance.
- The car became another surface for ambient AI interaction.

## Why it matters

Vehicles are high-context environments where convenience and safety collide. The success of automotive AI will depend on low-friction voice interaction, reliable grounding, and careful limits on distraction.
