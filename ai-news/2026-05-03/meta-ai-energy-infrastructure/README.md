# 2026-05-03 - Meta ties AI infrastructure to new energy systems

Meta's energy partnerships showed that AI infrastructure planning now reaches into generation and storage.

## What happened

- Meta explored large-scale space solar and long-duration storage partnerships.
- AI data center growth made energy availability a strategic constraint.
- Infrastructure planning expanded beyond chips and servers.

## Why it matters

AI scale is becoming an energy systems problem. Companies that can secure cleaner, cheaper, and more reliable power may gain a durable advantage in model training and inference economics.
